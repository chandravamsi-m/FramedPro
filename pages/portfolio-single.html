Team Shoot

80 Executives, One Day, 38-Hour Delivery

How Vantage Group HR coordinated an entire company rebrand photoshoot using the FramedPro client portal — with zero email chaos and 100% team approval.

The Challenge

Vantage Group had just completed a major brand refresh. Their existing team headshots were three to six years old and completely inconsistent — a mix of office selfies, outdated white-background shots, and a few good headshots that made the inconsistency even more obvious.

HR Director Sarah Chen needed to photograph 80 people across two departments in a single day, manage the approval workflow without her inbox exploding, and have everyone's photos ready within 72 hours due to a board presentation the following week.

What We Did

  • 01

    Set up two shooting bays in adjacent conference rooms

    Two-bay setup allowed us to photograph two people simultaneously, cutting the single-day session time from 11 hours to 6.5 hours.

  • 02

    Provided HR with a scheduling template

    A shared sign-up sheet let employees book their 6-minute slot autonomously. HR had full oversight without chasing anyone manually.

  • 03

    Delivered proofed gallery in 38 hours

    All 80 gallery sets were fully edited and uploaded to the client portal within 38 hours of the shoot day wrapping.

  • 04

    100% approval through the portal in 6 hours

    Sarah sent one team-wide notification. All 80 employees reviewed and approved their photos in under 6 hours. Zero back-and-forth with HR.
