About

The story behind the lens

Building a business where corporate photography means something more than just a decent headshot.

400+

Corporate clients served

Hi, I'm Marcus Reid.

I started FramedPro out of a frustration shared by every HR manager I'd ever spoken to: corporate headshot days are completely unnecessarily chaotic. Scheduling nightmares, files delivered weeks late, no clear way to review or download images.

So I built a process that actually works. Streamlined shoot days, 48-hour gallery delivery, and a client portal that HR teams can hand off to their whole department without a training manual.

Over the past 12 years, I've photographed partners at law firms, executives at hedge funds, doctors at hospital groups, and entire engineering departments at software companies. The work varies; the standard doesn't.

Studio philosophy

Three principles that guide every shoot, every delivery, every client interaction.

Speed is a feature

Turnaround time is part of the product. A headshot that arrives in 48 hours is more valuable than a technically perfect one that arrives in two weeks.

Precision over volume

We take on a limited number of clients per month. This means consistent quality, consistent availability, and a team that actually knows your account.

HR-first workflow

The portal, the scheduling, the file formats — everything is designed around how HR teams actually work, not around how photographers prefer to deliver.
