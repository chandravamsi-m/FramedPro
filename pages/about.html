The Founder

Hi, I'm Marcus Reid.

I founded FramedPro to solve a fundamental problem: corporate photography was stuck in 1995. HR managers were battling archaic scheduling, slow delivery, and unpredictable quality.

I spent a decade refining a **workflow-first** approach. Today, we don't just provide headshots; we provide a high-velocity visual engine that helps companies like yours project absolute authority in 48 hours or less.

From partners at top-tier law firms to engineering leads at Silicon Valley startups, I've had the privilege of capturing over 8,000 professionals. The scale has changed, but the obsession with the perfect frame remains the same.
